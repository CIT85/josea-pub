About this site


Summary

What is this site about?

This site is dedicated to showcasing the diverse landscapes of the world. From towering mountains to vast deserts, serene bodies of water, cold regions, and lush forests, each page provides a glimpse into the beauty and variety of our planet's natural environments.


Who is it for?

This site is for anyone interested in exploring the natural beauty of our planet. Whether you are a student, a traveler, or simply someone who appreciates nature, you will find some valuable information and great visuals on each page.


What inspired me to make this?

I was inspired by looking at posts about serene views of different landscapes on social media platforms, especially on TikTok! (I've also seen city landscapes, not just natural ones.)


Future Plans

I may or may not add more information to most of the files. But I am thinking about adding smooth scrolling behavior to all html files sooner or later. I could keep the local version of this repository and maybe add JavaScript for enhanced interactivity (I'll do that once I've learned how to use that coding language).
